The following information is needed for Vehicle Operations that need gudiance in the daily cleaning of their vehicle to reduce teh potential rist of spreading germs and viruses. The cleaning should be performed by the vehicle operator at the end of the shift after parking the vehicle.

What should be cleaned
  • Steering wheel
  • Shifter/turn signal/wiper controls
  • Function control buttons
  • Door handels
  • Armrests
  • Dashboards/Glovebox
  • Seatbelts/buckle/release button
  • Frequently touched items and surfaces

Instructions for Cleaning
  • Option 1
    If available use bleach based or alcohol based wipes
  • Option 2
    If bleach and alcohol wipes are not available, dampen (not wet/dripping) a cloth or paper towel with water and a small amount of soap/liquid soap

  • Perform wip down, taking care around electronics
  • Follow up with a dry wipe down with a paper towel
  • Dispose of wipes/paper towels in a sealed bag or container (both options)
  • Thoroughly wash your hands with soap and water or use hand sanitizer afer performing option 1 or 2
