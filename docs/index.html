Winter Slips and Falls

A significant amount of our injuries are due to slips and falls. Injuries sustained from a fall are among the most expensive and timely for recovery.

  • Wear the appropriate winter footwear, including yak traks/over boots.
  • When there is a potential for a slip/fall, use the D-ring ladder carry.
  • Slow down and make time to proceed safely.
  • When entering or exiting your vehicle, do so with caution and control
  • Eliminate slipping hazards around your work area, if possible.
  • Be aware of your surroundings. Stay focused on walking (not texting).
  • Don’t carry things in a way that hinders your vision.
  • If walking on ice, walk like a penguin.
  • Be prepared to fall. And if you do, hunch so you don’t hit your head.
